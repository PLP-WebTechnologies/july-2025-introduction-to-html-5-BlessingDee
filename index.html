My View Of the World

My perspective of how the world works differs from several people. I once referenced a song by Sasha Alex Sloan, "Santa's Real".

Her lyrics talked about how she wished we lived in a world where nobody fell sick and nobody suffered. It was an emotional point of view and I could easily tap into that feeling.

The world is such a painful space. Despite the merry, the goodness, I think the world breaks us more than we know.
